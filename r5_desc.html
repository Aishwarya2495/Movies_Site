Description : This movie begins with Madhav Jha (Arjun Kapoor) who gets admission in a college through sports quota. He meets Riya Somani (Shraddha Kapoor) and falls into love with her at their first Meeting. Riya, however wants to maintain a good friendship with Madhav Jha. Madhav forced by his friends, tries to force Riya into being his girlfriend and being physical . Riya gets emotionally hurt and moves apart. Madhav Jha apologises for his deed but hereby ignored Riya because of his unfit attempt.
