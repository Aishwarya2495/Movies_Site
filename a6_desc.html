Date of Release : December 15, 2017

Cast :
  1. John Cena as Ferdinand the Bull
  2. Kate McKinnon as Lupe, an old goat
  3. Gina Rodriguez as Una, a hedgehog
  4. Daveed Diggs as Dos, a hedgehog
  5. Gabriel Iglesias as Cuatro, a hedgehog



Description : Ferdinand is a Spanish Fighting Bull who prefers smelling the flowers and practicing non-violence rather than chasing red cloths held by matadors in arenas. But when disaster comes to him, he is taken to a fighting stadium like every bull in Spain, and Ferdinand needs to decide if he is a fighting bull or a flower smelling, generous bull, in order to earn his freedom.
