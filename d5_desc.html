Description : Simran is an upcoming Indian Bollywood drama film directed by Hansal Mehta which features Kangana Ranaut in the lead role.[1][2] Production began in March 2016 and principal photography commenced in October 2016 at Atlanta, United States. The film is scheduled for a worldwide release on 15 September 2017.[3]
